FAQ / The Basics

Questions we hear.

If your question is not here, call us. We pick up between sessions.

How do I do a drop in?

Call us, email us, or show up ten minutes before any coached session. First time drop ins fill out a one page waiver. Twenty five bucks, cash or card. You can join any posted class.

Do I need experience?

No. Coaches teach every movement before you load the bar. If you have been lifting for a while, bring your numbers. If this is your first barbell, we will start with PVC.

Can I cancel my membership?

Anytime, end of your current month. No cancellation fee. Email us or stop in. No phone trees.

What is a programming consult?

Thirty minutes with Priya. She looks at your training history, your goals, and your schedule. You walk out with a written twelve week plan. Unlimited members get one free per year. Non members can book one for 75 dollars.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes. Small group and one on one. Ask at the front desk for current coach availability.

Is there a student discount?

Twenty five dollars off Monthly or Unlimited with valid UT Austin student ID, every month you are enrolled.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, up to two months per year. Travel, injury, whatever. Email us two weeks ahead if possible.
