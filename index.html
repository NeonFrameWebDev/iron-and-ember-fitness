No. 01 / Why We Built It

A gym for people who came to lift.

Austin has plenty of places to post a selfie. Iron and Ember is not one of them.

Carlos Medina opened Iron and Ember in 2019 with four squat racks, a platform, and a stopwatch. He was done with gyms that cared more about playlists than programming.

Today the room has twelve platforms, a full Olympic area, and a bench of coaches who live on the floor. Programming is periodized. Every member gets greeted by name. Nobody's teaching you to dance.
